This is a demo version

How to use

1) Download this repository as a zip and extract it
2) Load chrome
3) Click on the burger menu (top right)
4) Click on "More Tools"
5) Click on "Extensions"
6) Click on "Load unpacked extension"
7) Do a search on twitter
8) Click on "all"
9) Click on the HumaBird Logo in the top right
10) Click on Search Harvest

Wait.....

Every 100 tweets it will download a text file of the tweets and stop when twitter has no more tweets
